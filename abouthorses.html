Breeds

Horses are one of the most diverse species of domesticated animals, with a rich variety of breeds developed over centuries. Currently, there are over 300 recognized horse breeds worldwide, each with unique characteristics, temperaments, and abilities. From the agile Thoroughbred, known for its speed on the racetrack, to the sturdy Clydesdale, renowned for its strength and gentle nature, these breeds reflect the various roles horses have played in human history, including work, sport, and companionship. This remarkable diversity not only highlights the adaptability of horses but also the deep bond between humans and these magnificent creatures.
